What We Offer

Services

Portrait

Intimate, authentic portraits that reveal character. Whether personal branding, family sessions, or editorial work — we capture the real you in light that feels natural and honest.

Wedding

Unobtrusive, documentary-style wedding coverage. We become invisible so you can live the moment freely — and find the photographs waiting for you on the other side.

Commercial

Product, architecture, and campaign photography built for brands with high standards. Clean, purposeful images that communicate value before a single word is read.

"Every photograph is a certificate of presence."

— Roland Barthes

Founded in 2014 by Elise Marchand, Lens & Light is a photography studio based in Copenhagen. We work with individuals, couples, and brands who believe images should carry weight — not just beauty. Our approach is slow, considered, and always rooted in the quality of light.

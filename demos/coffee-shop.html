Our Story

Roasted with intention

Brew & Co started in a tiny warehouse on Maple Street with one roaster, two espresso machines, and a stubborn belief that great coffee changes your day. Thirteen years later, not much has changed — except the size of the queue.

We work directly with smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every harvest is cupped, approved, and roasted to order. When you taste the difference, you'll understand why we bother.
